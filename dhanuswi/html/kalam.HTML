About Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. Known as the "Missile Man of India," he was celebrated for his work in India's civilian space and military missile programs. His contributions continue to inspire millions.
